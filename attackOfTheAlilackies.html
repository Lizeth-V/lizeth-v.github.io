Attack of the Alilackies!

Attack of the Alilackies is a fast-paced fantasy 3D first-person shooter inspired by classics like Doom and Dusk. Trapped in a world under siege, you must battle relentless Alilakeys to escape their invasion.

I was responsible for programming enemy AI, ensuring the Alilakeys moved, attacked, and reacted dynamically to player actions by switching through animations, making combat feel engaging.
